Our Story

Founded in 2012, SoundSphere began with a simple mission: to bring high-quality sound experiences to everyone, regardless of budget or technical expertise.

Our journey started in a small garage in San Francisco, where our founders, Alex Chen and Maya Rodriguez, two audio engineers with a passion for music, began developing prototype headphones that would deliver premium sound without the premium price tag.

Today, SoundSphere has grown into a global brand with over 500 employees worldwide, but our core mission remains unchanged. We continue to push the boundaries of audio technology while remaining committed to accessibility, quality, and sustainability.

Our Mission

At SoundSphere, we're on a mission to transform how people experience sound. We believe that exceptional audio should be accessible to everyone, and we're dedicated to creating products that deliver on that promise.

Through continuous innovation and attention to detail, we strive to bring the joy of immersive sound to people around the world, enhancing how they experience music, podcasts, gaming, and more.

Our Values

Innovation

We continuously push the boundaries of audio technology, seeking new ways to improve sound quality and user experience.

Quality

We hold ourselves to the highest standards in design, manufacturing, and customer service to ensure lasting value.

Accessibility

We believe everyone deserves great sound, so we create products at various price points without compromising quality.

Sustainability

We're committed to reducing our environmental footprint through responsible manufacturing and recyclable materials.
